Persian salad with Pomegranate

Ingredients Quantity
Persian cucumber 2
cherry tomatoes 1 cup
red onion 1/2
red bell pepper 1
fresh parsley 1/4 cup
fresh mint 1/4 cup
pomegranate seeds 1/2 cup
pomegranate molasses dressing 4 tbs

Step 1:dice all the vegetables small and place them into a large mixing bowl. Get a pomegranate and take out seeds from it and then add it into other vegetables.

Step 2:finely shop the parsley and mint. Add them to the bowl.

Step 3:make a pomegranate dressing mixing pomegranate molasses, olive oil, honey, garlic, mustard, salt, and pepper.
